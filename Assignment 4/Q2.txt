Q -> HTML entities ? 
ans -> HTML entities are used to to add/use special characters and symbols , that have reserved in HTML syntax .
	This provide wide range of charaters , allowing us to add icons , geometric shapes , mathematical operators
	and so on which are not present on keyboard.
	
	Commonly used entity->
	1 -> &alt = <(less than)
	2 -> &gt = >(gretaer than)
	3 -> &nbsp = non-breaking space
	4 -> &amp = &(ampersand)
	5 -> &copy = copyright symbool
	
	We can use 3 entities to represent single character and symbols . Hexadecimal numbers , decimal numbers and by name.
